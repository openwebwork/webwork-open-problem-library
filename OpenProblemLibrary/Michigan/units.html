units cribsheet

In general, use the units specified in the problem. Represent units as indicated in the table below, including them separated from your answer by a space (also, be sure there are no spaces at the end of your units). Note that cApITaliZaTiON matters. Enter compound units with only one "/", using * for multiplication and ^ for exponentiation. For example, the following are all correct ways to enter a value with compound units: 5 m/s^2,   7.38 kg*m/s^2,   12 V*ohm^-1*s^-1   and 16 V/ohm*s.
